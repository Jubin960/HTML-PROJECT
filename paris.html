About Paris

Paris is known for its stunning architecture, famous landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, and a rich cultural heritage. As the capital of France, it's a hub of art, fashion, and cuisine. Whether you're visiting the Louvre or walking down the Champs-Élysées, Paris offers something for everyone.

Top Attractions:

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica
  • Champs-Élysées

Best Time to Visit:

Spring (April to June) and Fall (September to November) are the best times to explore Paris, as the weather is mild and pleasant.
