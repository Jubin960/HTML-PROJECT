Cultural Treasures of Kyoto

Kyoto, Japan's ancient capital, is a city steeped in history, with over 1,600 Buddhist temples, stunning shrines, and traditional tea houses. A visit to Kyoto is a journey through Japan’s cultural heart, where tradition meets tranquility.

Must-Visit Temples and Shrines

  • Fushimi Inari Shrine: Famous for its thousands of red torii gates leading up the mountain.
  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion): A stunning Zen temple covered in gold leaf, reflecting beautifully in its surrounding pond.
  • Kiyomizu-dera: A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its wooden stage offering stunning views of the city.

Experiencing Kyoto’s Culture

While in Kyoto, participate in a traditional tea ceremony, stroll through the bamboo groves of Arashiyama, or visit a geisha district to see Japan's living cultural heritage.
