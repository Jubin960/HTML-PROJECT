About Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan’s bustling capital, is known for its skyscrapers, shopping, and vibrant nightlife. It's a city where modernity meets tradition, with ancient temples standing tall among neon-lit buildings.

Top Attractions:

  • Shibuya Crossing
  • Sensō-ji Temple
  • Tokyo Skytree
  • Meiji Shrine
  • Ginza District

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Tokyo is during spring (March to May) when the cherry blossoms are in full bloom, and autumn (September to November) when the city is alive with vibrant fall colors.
